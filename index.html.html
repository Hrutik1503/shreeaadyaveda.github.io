Panchakarma Treatments

The Five Actions of Deep Purification and Rejuvenation

Panchakarma is the ultimate mind-body healing experience for detoxifying the body, strengthening the immune system, and restoring balance and well-being. It is a cornerstone of Ayurvedic medicine and consists of five therapeutic treatments.

Vamana (Emesis Therapy)

Therapeutic vomiting to eliminate excess Kapha dosha and toxins from the upper body. Effective for respiratory and skin disorders.

Virechana (Purgation Therapy)

Controlled purgation to cleanse the gastrointestinal tract and eliminate Pitta dosha. Beneficial for liver and digestive disorders.

Basti (Enema Therapy)

Medicated enema treatment to balance Vata dosha and cleanse the colon. Highly effective for neurological and joint disorders.

Nasya (Nasal Administration)

Herbal oil or powder administered through the nasal passage to clear sinuses and treat head and neck disorders.

Raktamokshana (Bloodletting)

Blood purification therapy to remove toxins from the bloodstream. Effective for skin diseases and blood disorders.
